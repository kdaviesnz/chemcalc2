Help

To synthesise a molecule:
ChemCalc > synthesize [molecule name]
Examples:
ChemCalc > synthesize oxidanium
ChemCalc > synthesize ethanol

To see list of commands:
ChemCalc > commands

To view flask:
ChemCalc > flask

To empty flask
ChemCalc > empty

To remove chemical from flask
ChemCalc > distill [chemical]

To add chemical to flask:
ChemCalc > add [n] [chemical]
Example:
ChemCalc > add 10 pinacol
ChemCalc > add 1 hydrochloric acid
